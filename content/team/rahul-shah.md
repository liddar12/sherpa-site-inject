With nearly 20 years in real estate lending, Rahul has been involved in the origination, structuring, underwriting, due diligence, and closing of more than $1 billion of commercial real estate transactions.

He began his career at National City Bank, where he underwrote and closed commercial real estate transactions across all property types. He then joined Inland Western Retail Real Estate Trust where he originated and underwrote commercial mortgage loans on retail assets nationally. Most recently, he co-founded Sherpa Capital Group.
