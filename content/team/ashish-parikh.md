With more than 18 years in real estate lending, Ashish has been involved in the origination, structuring, underwriting, due diligence, and closing of more than $2 billion of commercial real estate transactions across all property types.

He began his career at LaSalle Bank/ABN AMRO, where he originated and structured loans for institutional investors and developers. With the desire to return to the production side of mortgage lending, he joined Inland Western Retail Real Estate Trust Inc, where he originated mortgage transactions on retail shopping centers throughout the country. Most recently, he co-founded Sherpa Capital Group.
