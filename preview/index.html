Sherpa Capital Group LLC is a boutique private equity real estate firm specializing in originating bridge debt and equity investments secured by commercial non-owner occupied real estate.

Founded in 2010 to capitalize on the lack of credit in the real estate industry, Sherpa was built by principals with a combined 35 years of experience in real estate lending. The firm delivers innovative creative lending solutions, diverse product offerings, and exceptional service.
